This is a general guide, not a substitute for tool manuals or training.

## PPE (personal protective equipment)

- **Eye protection**: always.
- **Hearing protection**: for saws, routers, shop vacs.
- **Dust protection**: especially for MDF and sanding.

## Power tool habits that prevent injuries

- Keep hands out of the line of cut.
- Clamp work whenever possible.
- Let blades stop fully before setting tools down.
- Unplug / remove battery before changing blades/bits.

## The 10-second pause

Before drilling or cutting, pause and confirm:

1. What is the **waste side**?
2. Is the work **supported**?
3. Is the cord/battery path clear?
4. Do I have a safe stance and good visibility?
