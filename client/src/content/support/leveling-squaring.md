When something "doesn’t fit," it’s often because it’s **not square**.

## Level vs square vs plumb

- **Level**: horizontal.
- **Plumb**: vertical.
- **Square**: 90° between parts.

## Checking for square

- Use a **combination square** at corners.
- Measure diagonals on a rectangle:
  - If diagonals match, it’s square.

## Fixing a racked frame

- Loosen fasteners slightly.
- Push/pull the frame until diagonals match.
- Re-tighten while holding position.

## Shimming and wobble

- Floors are rarely perfectly flat.
- Use **shims** under legs/corners and trim excess.
